Frequently Asked Questions

How to register?

You can simply go to our landing page and fill in a form. Your account will be automatically created.

How to settle the payment?

Potato Fintech has tried our best to provide the ways for settling payment as much as possible. Currently, we accepting Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, and JCB) and direct debit.

How the direct debit works?

Although there would be lots of stuff have to deal with setting up the direct debit, but for you that would be just a banana as we are using our advanced workflow system to process it automatically. You only need to authorize us to kick off the process. We currently only support Hong Kong local bank's account for setting up the direct debit function.
