The Potato Team

We are a team of student from the City University of Hong Kong that would like to organise different types of activities. Everyday, we organised more than 1 activity each week to student inside CityU and other higher insitutional students to join. We cover different types of activities from food party to night party, interest group to hiking.

Although we have a bunch of different types of activities, it is difficult for us to let our potential participants to know. By using the Potato Fintech CS3382 CityU HK's solution, the Potato, it is more easy to let other student to know our upcoming events as it is directly linked to Facebook's API. User can see the same piece of information across 2 sites. Besides, the Potato's solution also bring a significant userful solution that it also provides a payment services. It helps us to collect the fees and membership annual fee automatically through various way including Credit Card and even direct debit on bank accounts.
